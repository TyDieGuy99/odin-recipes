Mississippi Pot Roast

Mississippi Pot Roast

Home Page

Super easy crockpot recipe that takes almost no effort to make!

Ma 8 servings and takes about 8 hours to cook!

Ingredients:

  • 1 (3-4 punds) chuck roast
  • 1 packet ranch dressing mix
  • 1 packet au jus gravy mix
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 4-5 pepperoncini peppers

Steps:

  1. Place roast in the slow cooker and sprinkle the top with the ranch dressing mix and the au jus mix. Place peppers on top of the mixture and add butter
  2. Cook over low heat for 8 hours. Serve with noodles, rice, or mashed potatoes.
